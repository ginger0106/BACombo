Leaf

A Benchmark for Federated Settings


Leaf is a benchmarking framework for learning in federated settings, with applications including federated learning, multi-task learning, meta-learning, and on-device learning.
Future releases will include additional tasks and datasets. Please contact Sebastian Caldas with questions or to contribute to the benchmark.
